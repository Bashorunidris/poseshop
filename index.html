PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Pose Shop, we collect your email address and user ID from your Pose account to process your purchase and credit coins to your wallet, plus transaction details for record-keeping.

2. How We Use Your Information

Used solely to process payments, credit coins, and provide support. We never sell or share your data for marketing.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by Flutterwave (card/bank). Pose does not store your card details.

4. Your Rights

Request access, correction, or deletion of your data anytime at poseinfos@gmail.com.
TERMS OF USE
Last updated: July 2026

1. Pose Coins

Pose Coins are a virtual in-app currency for use exclusively within the Pose app. They cannot be transferred, resold, withdrawn, or exchanged for cash.

2. Purchases Are Final

All purchases are non-refundable once coins are credited.

3. Regional Pricing

Coin prices vary by country to reflect local purchasing power and payment processing costs. All prices are set in Naira and converted to your local currency at a fixed rate that Pose updates periodically — not a live market rate — to keep prices stable.

4. Service Fee

A service fee is added to every purchase to cover payment processing (4% Nigeria, 6% other African countries, 10% elsewhere), calculated on the coin price before any bonus.

5. Bonus Coins

Select packages include bonus coins at no extra cost, shown before you pay.

6. Contact

Questions? Email poseinfos@gmail.com.
