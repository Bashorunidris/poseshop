Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Pose Shop, we collect your email address and user ID from your Pose account in order to process your purchase and credit coins to your wallet. We also collect transaction details such as amount paid, coins purchased, and payment reference for record-keeping purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to process payments, credit Pose Coins to your account, and provide customer support. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed securely by Flutterwave (card/bank) and Breet (crypto/stablecoin). Pose does not store your card or wallet details. All payment data is handled by our payment partners in accordance with their respective security standards.

4. Breet Integration

When you choose to pay with Breet, your transaction data is processed by Breet in accordance with Breet's Privacy Policy. Pose receives only transaction confirmation and amount to credit your coins. No crypto wallet details are stored by Pose.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at poseinfos@gmail.com.

What is Breet?

Breet is a crypto and stablecoin payment platform that allows users to make fast, secure global payments using digital currencies including USDT, USDC, Bitcoin, Ethereum and more.

Every purchase made through Breet on the Pose Shop earns 10% of the purchase amount back as Pose Coins. This is automatic — no codes, no claims. The cashback is credited to your Pose wallet immediately after your payment confirms. Programme runs for the first 6 months.

First Deposit Bonus

When you fund your wallet or make your first purchase through Breet, you receive a one-time bonus worth 10% of that first transaction in Pose Coins. This is a welcome reward and applies only once per account.

⚡ Creator Withdrawal Bonus

Creators who choose to withdraw their Pose earnings through Breet receive reduced withdrawal fees or bonus Pose Coins on their first three withdrawals. This encourages creators to experience faster, borderless payouts.

Breet enables Pose users around the world — including those without Nigerian bank accounts — to purchase Pose Coins using their crypto wallets. This makes the Pose economy truly global and accessible to every creator's audience, wherever they are.

Simply select "Pay with Breet" in the payment method section above before completing your purchase. All rewards are applied automatically. No registration or extra steps needed.
